Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our research institute

What is the Research Institute?

The Research Institute is a leading research organization dedicated to advancing knowledge through innovative research across multiple disciplines including genomics, AI, climate science, and quantum computing.

How can I access research data?

Research data and datasets are available through our Datasets section. Some datasets may require registration or approval depending on the data type and usage restrictions.

How can I collaborate with the institute?

We welcome collaboration opportunities. Please visit our Contact page and select "Collaboration Request" to submit a proposal. Our team will review and respond to your inquiry.

How do I cite your publications?

Each publication page includes citation buttons for APA, MLA, and Chicago formats. You can also export citations in BibTeX or RIS format for reference management software.

Are your publications open access?

Many of our publications are open access. Check individual publication pages for access information. PDF downloads are available for most publications.

Can I visit your research facilities?

We offer facility tours by appointment. Please contact us through our Contact page to schedule a visit. We also host open house events throughout the year.
